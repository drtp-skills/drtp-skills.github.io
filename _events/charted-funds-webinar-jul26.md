The CHARTED project team will host a webinar on Thursday 23rd July 2026, 15:00 BST, to provide a brief
overview of the project and introduce two new funds accepting applications over the coming months - 
[Fund 1 (FAIRifying Training Resources)](https://drtp-skills.ac.uk/funds-fair/) and [Fund 3 (Tools and Frameworks)](https://drtp-skills.ac.uk/funds-tools/).
The team will also provide a brief summary of the two existing [funds](https://drtp-skills.ac.uk/funds/) - Fund 2 (Community activities)
and Fund 4 (Professional development) - which will continue to accept applications.

The webinar will also provide details of how to apply to the CHARTED funds via the GoodGrants platform
and provide an opportunity for attendees to ask questions about the funds and the application process.
