## Information about you: how we use it and with whom we share it

The information you provide will be used by the CHARTED project (a collaborative project between the University of Edinburgh, Imperial College London, and the University of Southampton) to:

- Keep you up to date with news and progress regarding the CHARTED project.
- Send you our regular digest of updated content from our website.
- Promote events, activities and programmes.
- Ensure we only communicate with you about events, opportunities, services or programmes of interest to you.

The CHARTED project will use an external company to process information about you on the project’s behalf. We use Mailchimp as our marketing platform. By subscribing to our communications, you acknowledge that your information will be transferred to Mailchimp for processing. [Learn more about Mailchimp's privacy practices here](https://mailchimp.com/legal/).


The CHARTED project, through its constituent University partners, remains responsible for the information and will ensure it is kept securely.

## Legal basis: consent

We are using information about you because you have given us your consent.

We will only contact you by email. You can change your mind at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in the footer of any email you receive from us, or by contacting Weronika Filinger at [w.filinger@epcc.ed.ac.uk](mailto:w.filinger@epcc.ed.ac.uk).

We will hold the personal data you provided until you choose to stop receiving marketing and communications from us, or one year after the closure of the CHARTED project.

If you have any questions, please contact: Weronika Filinger, Project Lead - CHARTED, at [w.filinger@epcc.ed.ac.uk](mailto:w.filinger@epcc.ed.ac.uk).

Read our full [Privacy Policy]({{ site.baseurl }}/privacy-policy/) on our website for more information on how we use your information.

The CHARTED project’s Privacy Policy is also supported by the University of Edinburgh's wider privacy policies and guidelines. The following pages contain information about mandatory data sharing, transfer outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and information on how to contact the University's Data Protection Officer.
